Screen Replacement and Battery Replacement

At Mobile World Station, we understand the importance of a fully functional device. Whether you're dealing with a cracked screen or a battery that struggles to hold a charge, our expert repair services ensure optimal performance and device longevity.

Signs You Need a Screen Replacement:

- Visible cracks on the Screen

- Distorted display or color abnormalities.

- Unresponsive touch functionality.

Signs You Need a Battery Replacement:

- Rapid battery drain.

- Unexpected device shutdowns.

- Overheating issues


Step-by-Step Guide: Screen Replacement

1. Safety Precautions: Our technicians prioritize safety with protective gear (safety glasses, gloves).

2. Tools and Materials: We use specialized tools (screwdrivers, spudger) and high-quality replacement screens.

3. Turn Off the Device: Before starting the replacement, we ensure the device is powered off.

4. Remove the Back Cover (if applicable): If applicable, our experts safely remove the back cover.

5. Disconnect the Battery: Ensuring a safe replacement process, we disconnect the battery.

6. Remove the Damaged Screen: Our skilled technicians safely detach the damaged screen, avoiding further harm.

7. Install the New Screen: Carefully installing the new screen, we ensure proper alignment and connection.

8. Reassemble the Device: Our professionals put the device back together, securing screws, and ensuring all components are in place.

9. Test the New Screen: We conduct thorough tests to ensure the new screen meets our quality standards.

10. Troubleshooting Tips: In case of any issues post-replacement, we provide expert troubleshooting tips.


Step-by-Step Guide: Battery Replacement

1. Safety Precautions: Our technicians prioritize safety with protective gear (safety glasses, gloves).

2. Tools and Materials: We use specialized tools (screwdrivers, spudger) and high-quality replacement batteries.

3. Turn Off the Device: Before starting the replacement, we ensure the device is powered off.

4. Remove the Back Cover (if applicable): If applicable, our experts safely remove the back cover.

5. Locate and Disconnect the Old Battery: Our skilled technicians identify and safely disconnect the old battery.

6. Install the New Battery: Carefully installing the new battery, we ensure proper alignment and connection.

9. Calibrate the Battery (if necessary): If needed, we perform battery calibration for optimal performance.
